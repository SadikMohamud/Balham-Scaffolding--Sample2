Complex Access Solutions for Challenging Sites

Our team excels at creating custom scaffolding solutions for buildings with unusual designs, limited access points, or restricted work areas

When standard scaffolding approaches won't work, we design bespoke access systems that overcome site limitations while maintaining the highest safety standards. We evaluate each unique challenge and develop innovative solutions using specialized components and advanced techniques to ensure safe access regardless of architectural complexity.

Weather Protection with Temporary Roof Systems

Our temporary roofing solutions provide complete weather protection for construction projects, enabling work to continue regardless of conditions

We design and install custom temporary roof systems that protect your site from rain, snow, and debris. These systems can span large areas without intermediate supports, feature integrated drainage systems, and can be modified with side sheeting for complete encapsulation when required.

Heritage and Listed Building Expertise

Specialized scaffolding services for historic buildings that require extra care and preservation considerations

Our heritage scaffolding team is trained in the unique requirements of listed buildings and restoration projects. We use specialized techniques to minimize impact on delicate structures, employ load-spreading foundations, and can design scaffolding that allows public access to remain open when required by museums and public buildings.

Heavy-Duty Load-Bearing Platforms

Engineered solutions for projects requiring material storage, equipment support, or workforce platforms

Our structural engineers design and implement load-bearing platforms that can support significant weight requirements. Whether you need platforms for material storage, demolition work, or to support heavy machinery, we calculate load distributions and design appropriate structures that integrate seamlessly with your main scaffolding system.
